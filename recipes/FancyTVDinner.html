Repas divin de grande personne

An expertly crafted meal for all ages to enjoy.

A delicious and nutricious TV Dinner. The dish comes with three well cared for chicken nuggets that have been cooked to perfection. There's corn, mac and cheese, and a couple of gummies that have been hand selected for maximum satisfaction.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Corn
  • A box of Kraft Macaroni and Cheese
  • Flintstones Gummies Complete
  • Animal Shaped Chicken Nuggies

Steps

  1. Aquire water and boil the water in a pot. Cover the pot afterwards.
  2. Cut the corn into pieces and drop them into the boiling hot water.
  3. Get the Flintstones Gummies Complete and open the container. If you can't, ask your Mother or Father to assist.
  4. Carefully choose the biggest gummies in the container. Make sure they are all different colors as well.
  5. Get the bag of Animal Shaped Chicken Nuggies.
  6. Take three chicken nuggies and throw them into the oven and read the instructions on how to cook them.
  7. Once everything is done, take all of that and place it on one plate and you're done!
