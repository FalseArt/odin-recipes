Le plat coloré de lait et de céréales

A sweet treat to finish off your dinner!

If you would enjoy a satisfying finale to your luxirous fine dining; look no further than this dessert.

It contains a bowl of fruit loops in a bowl of milk. Now if you're feeling extra fancy, we can substitute it for 2% milk with no charge! And for those on a diet, we offer water instead of the milk for a $10 fee.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Fruit Loops
  • Optional: Spoon

Steps

  1. Get a bowl and place the milk in first.
  2. Afterwards, put the fruit loops into the bowl. Make sure you put the fruit loops after the milk is already in the bowl.
  3. Get a spoon and serve!
