Le bol chaud de nouilles

A hot bowl to warm up your spirits!

A hot meal filled with complete bliss and flavor. Look no further than our bowl of instant hot noodles if you're looking for something truly out of this world. The noodles are prepared in the most pure water out there that is boiled. It's sourced from the tropical Fiji islands.

Shrimp flavor is also avaliable for patrons as well.

Ingredients

  • Tap water
  • Maruchan Ramen Noodle Soup

Steps

  1. Get a bowl and fill it with tap water. Then boil the water.
  2. Afterwards, extract the instant noodles and slam it into the boiling hot water.
  3. Take the flavored powder and dump it inside the boiling hot pot of water with the noodles.
  4. Stir everything together.
  5. Enjoy!
